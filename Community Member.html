An Active Community Member

Empowering Businesses and Communities

Beyond my professional achievements, I am deeply committed to community engagement. As a mentor to aspiring professionals and a supporter of local businesses, I strive to foster a culture of growth, learning, and shared success. My involvement on boards such as BC Soccer, the Richmond Girls Soccer Association, and the Richmond Sports Council reflects my dedication to making a meaningful, lasting impact in the communities I serve.

Volunteer Leadership

Beyond corporate leadership, I have a deep passion for young people and leadership development. I have volunteered extensively with organizations such as the Boys and Girls Clubs, YMCA/YWCA, Junior Achievement, and universities, helping mentor the next generation of leaders.

Key Areas of Volunteer Leadership:

  • Chairing governance, judicial, and high-performance committees

  • Organizing dozens of major sporting events including regional and international tournaments

  • Mentoring young leaders through Boys and Girls Clubs, YMCA/YWCA, and Junior Achievement

Board and Advisory Roles

I have extensive experience as a board member and special advisor across multiple sectors, including corporate, non-profit, and community organizations. My expertise in governance, mergers and acquisitions, strategic planning, and operational excellence makes me an invaluable asset to organizations seeking impactful leadership.
