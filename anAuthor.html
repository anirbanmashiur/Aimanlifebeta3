RULE NUMBER ONE

In Rule No. 1: Nine Less-Than-Obvious Ways to Enhance Your Personal and Professional Life with Lessons from History, I draw on my lifelong passion for history to reveal how the decisions and behaviors of extraordinary individuals throughout time can guide our own leadership journeys. This book is not about fate—it's about agency. Through a blend of historical storytelling and real-world application, I present nine unconventional yet practical strategies that empower readers to lead with clarity, act with courage, and think beyond the obvious.

Each chapter explores a powerful insight—rooted in historical context and designed for modern impact—ending with a call to action that encourages personal and professional transformation. Whether you're leading an organization, building a career, or striving for personal excellence, Rule No. 1 offers a fresh, thought-provoking lens on what it takes to create meaningful, lasting change.
