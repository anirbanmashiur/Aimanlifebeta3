Art & Artist

Art has always been a mainstay of my lifestyle. Some of my earliest memories is sitting on my fathers lap at the age of probably two drawing dogs. I drew all through my early school days, achieving top recognition at high school. I took additional study at Emily Carr University of Art and Design and Langara College. Since then I have consistently pursued art as a hobby and occasionally as part of my profession. For many years I attend the Richmond Artist Guild life drawing sessions and that helped define some of my current style. Running and working in marketing departments for the last thirty years means that I am never far from the creative process. And I am always happy to lend my artistic and graphic expertise to friends in business and not for profits. These days I am dabbling in art more as I take the time to further perfect my craft.
